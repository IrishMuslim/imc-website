About Us

Who we are and what we stand for

The Irish Muslim Circle (IMC) is a community-driven organisation dedicated to serving the Irish Muslim community. We strive to strengthen the bonds of faith among Muslims living in Ireland while encouraging active participation in and contribution to Irish society.

Our work is guided by our core values:

Unity

Bringing the community together regardless of background or ethnicity.

Faith

Strengthening our connection with Allah and nurturing spiritual growth.

Discipline

Upholding integrity, responsibility, and excellence in all we do.
